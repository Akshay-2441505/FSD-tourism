Our Story

Founded in 1996 in Amsterdam, Booking.com has grown from a small Dutch startup to one of the world’s leading digital travel companies.

Booking.com seamlessly connects millions of travelers with memorable experiences, transport options, and incredible places to stay. No matter where you want to go, Booking.com makes it easy and backs it all up with 24/7 customer support.

"With over 265,000 participating hotels and accommodations in 178 countries, we believe Booking.com creates more of these incredible moments for people than anybody else"
